Prof. Nitin Zinzurke

Assistant Professor
Department of Computer Engineering

With over 16 years of teaching experience, Prof. Nitin Zinzurke has been serving as an Assistant Professor at JSPM’s Jayawantrao Sawant College of Engineering.

Throughout his career, he has taught a diverse range of subjects, including Data Science & Big Data Analytics, Internet Programming, Cloud Computing, Computer Networks, and Computer Architecture & Organization across both diploma and undergraduate programs.

Prof. Zinzurke is known for his student-centric teaching philosophy, which emphasizes strong theoretical foundations coupled with real-world applications, fostering a well-rounded learning environment that nurtures academic and practical excellence.
